Email / LINE / WhatsApp / Facebook Notes

Many hospitals change their digital contact channels over time. Some list direct email addresses, some use contact forms, and some prefer LINE or Facebook Messenger for quick patient support.

Best Practice

  • Go to the official website
  • Open Contact, International Patients, or Appointment
  • Check if they provide:
    • Email address
    • LINE official account
    • WhatsApp number
    • Facebook page / Messenger
    • Online appointment form
  • When contacting them, ask for:
    • doctor recommendation
    • consultation fee
    • earliest appointment
    • Myanmar interpreter availability

How to Book an Appointment

Step-by-Step

  1. Choose the hospital based on your condition.
  2. Open the hospital website.
  3. Use “Find a Doctor” and select specialty.
  4. Prepare your medical records in PDF or photo format.
  5. Contact the hospital via phone, email, form, LINE, or WhatsApp.
  6. Request Myanmar interpreter if needed.
  7. Confirm appointment date, fee, and what documents to bring.
